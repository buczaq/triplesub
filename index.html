⚓ MISSION BRIEFING
The year is 2089, and the militarized cartel known as KRAKEN INC. has seized the world's critical sea lanes using a vast experimental deep-sonar network. Your submarine — designation UBS TRITON-7 — is the only vessel capable of operating undetected inside KRAKEN's exclusion zones. Command has assigned you a series of precision strike missions that, if successful, will expose KRAKEN's command structure and liberate the blockaded free nations. KRAKEN's hunt drones will pursue any submarine that lingers — and each patrol is smarter and more relentless than the last. Complete your missions, outrun the hunters, and if cornered… fight.
