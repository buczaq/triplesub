HOW TO PLAY
THROW REAL DARTS · ENTER RESULTS · SURVIVE THE HUNT
COMPLETE 3 MISSION OBJECTIVES PER MISSION TO EARN XP
+2 XP BONUS PER DOUBLE · +3 XP BONUS PER TRIPLE (thrown in mission rounds)
AFTER EVERY 2 MISSIONS: SCORING ROUND (9 DARTS, XP = SCORE ÷ 10)
THEN: HUNT — SURVIVE IF YOUR XP EXCEEDS THE THRESHOLD
IF CAUGHT: 301 DUEL (SINGLE-IN, SINGLE-OUT) — WIN OR DIE
⚓ MISSION BRIEFING
The year is 2089, and the militarized cartel known as KRAKEN INC. has seized the world's critical sea lanes using a vast experimental deep-sonar network. Your submarine — designation UBS TRITON-7 — is the only vessel capable of operating undetected inside KRAKEN's exclusion zones. Command has assigned you a series of precision strike missions that, if successful, will expose KRAKEN's command structure and liberate the blockaded free nations. KRAKEN's hunt drones pursue any submarine that lingers — and each patrol is smarter and more relentless than the last. Complete your missions, outrun the hunters, and if cornered… fight.
